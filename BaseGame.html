What is Hitman WOA?

Hitman World Of Assassination is a stealth game where a superhuman assassin must find different ways to assassinate a target without being spotted wether its through mission stories or gunning down hundreds of innocent civillians, it is available through Xbox, Playstation, Nintento Switch, Linux and PC.

Who are the targets?

Throughout the game, there are many targets that you must kill, there are temporary targets that are playable for 48 hours once every year, targets that you can assassinate anytime and player-made contracts which are made by players in the community if they want their own story or want you to complete the mission as fast as possible.The targets are normally picked by your associate Diana Burnwood (mentioned in Agent 47) who deals with clients requests, most targets are arms dealers, terrorists, industrial scam artists etc.

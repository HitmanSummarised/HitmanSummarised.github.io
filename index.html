What is Hitman??

Hitman is a game series which begun on Nov 19th 2000, throughout the years the game has had many different stories, locations, characters and developers, the game now currently rests in the hands of IO Interactive, there are many different hitman games including Codename 47, Blood Money, Sniper Assassin, Absolution, WOA etc. The images above are covers of some of the older games.
